# HR-Analytics-communication
: a comprehensive Customer Churn Analysis for a Telecommunications provider. By leveraging data visualization, I’ve uncovered the key drivers behind customer attrition.

Key highlights from this dashboard:

Contract Risk: Identifying that month-to-month users have a churn rate 7x higher than yearly subscribers.

Behavioral Trends: Mapping how payment methods (71% Direct Debit) and data usage patterns influence loyalty.

Demographic Insights: Pinpointing the 'Age Bins' most likely to leave, allowing for targeted retention campaigns.

Data isn't just numbers; it’s a story about customer behavior. This dashboard helps stakeholders move from 'guessing' to 'knowing' where to focus their efforts."

الخيار الثاني: تحليلي (Focus on Problem-Solving)
العنوان: Why do customers leave? Let the data speak. 🔍

"Churn is the silent killer of growth. In this project, I dived deep into a dataset of 7,000 customers to answer the 'Why' behind the '26.86% churn rate'.

Through this Power BI dashboard, I analyzed:

Churn Reasons: Competitor offers and support quality are the primary triggers.

Product-Market Fit: Discovering that low-data users on 'Unlimited Plans' are a high-risk segment.

Customer Service Impact: Correlation between support calls and attrition across different age groups.

Turning complex datasets into clear, visual stories is what I do best. Check out the slides to see the full breakdown!"
